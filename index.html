Challenges with Media Tools

Attention Fragmentation

The design of numerous media platforms enables them to maintain user attention through constant operation, which results in breakdowns of concentration and makes it challenging to maintain ongoing involvement.

Context Switching Across Devices

Experiencing media between televisions, laptops, and mobile phones causes media experiences to break up and the very idea of deliberate consumption gets eroded during the process.

Blurring of Work and Leisure

The devices the email and LinkedIn account were and are used also for sharing personal thoughts, so there is no line drawn where work ends and one goes home.
